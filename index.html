RANJEENI @ GEENIE'S MEMORIAL

03.05.1980 ~ 23.10.2021

About Geenie

A hero who have passed away while fighting the infamous COVID-19. She have suffered alot since she was young but only her strength and will power brought her this far.

Biography

Married to an irresponsible husband, Geenie gave birth to 2 sons. She raised them as a single mother even though the kids still had their father. She divorced the man and continued to fight for her sons. She makes sure that her sons has enough basic needs even though she doesn't have food to eat and works regardless of her health condition. On 2017, Geenie married to her love of her life and started her new journey. Fighting all the odds, she raised both of her sons by teaching them how to behave, survive and what to do in this world. On 6th October 2021, Geenie was diagnosed positive for COVID-19. She suffered alot since she had Asthma which made her situation more worse. On 23rd October 2021, 11:16AM, Geenie passed away due to severe pneumonia and Anemia at Hospital Sungai Buloh.
